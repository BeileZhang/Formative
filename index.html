LOUIS VUITTON Exhibition at ESPACE Beijing

The works selected for this exhibition come from the Louis Vuitton Foundation's collection and are presented within the framework of the Foundation's Beyond the Walls project. The "Beyond the Walls" project is taking place in the Louis Vuitton Espace culture and art spaces in Tokyo, Munich, Venice, Beijing, Seoul and Osaka, in line with the Foundation's mission to invite a wider international public to participate in the Foundation's activities.
